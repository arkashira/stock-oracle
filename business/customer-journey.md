 # Customer-Journey.md

## Aware

### Trigger Event
Potential customer encounters a need for an AI-powered inventory replenishment planning tool while managing high-SKU DTC retail operations.

### Friction Points
- Lack of awareness about the existence of a solution tailored to their specific pain point.
- Difficulty understanding the benefits and unique features of the stock-oracle tool.

### User Emotions
- Frustration due to inefficient inventory management.
- Curiosity about potential solutions.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Clear and concise communication about the benefits and unique features of stock-oracle.
- Success stories or case studies from similar high-SKU DTC retailers.

### Metric
- Awareness level among high-SKU DTC retailers.

## Consider

### Trigger Event
Potential customer researches AI-powered inventory solutions to address their pain point.

### Friction Points
- Overwhelming number of inventory management solutions available.
- Difficulty comparing features, benefits, and pricing of different tools.

### User Emotions
- Skepticism about the effectiveness of AI-powered inventory tools.
- Anxiety about the potential cost and implementation effort.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Clear and easy-to-understand comparison of stock-oracle's features and benefits with competitors.
- Demonstration of the tool's effectiveness through live demos or free trials.

### Metric
- Number of potential customers considering stock-oracle.

## Try

### Trigger Event
Potential customer signs up for a free trial of stock-oracle.

### Friction Points
- Complexity of setting up the tool with multiple fulfillment centers and vendors.
- Difficulty understanding how to use the tool effectively.

### User Emotions
- Excitement about the potential benefits of the tool.
- Apprehension about the learning curve and implementation effort.

### Opportunities to Delight
- User-friendly onboarding process.
- Responsive customer support during the trial period.

### Metric
- Number of users who complete the free trial.

## Adopt

### Trigger Event
Potential customer decides to purchase stock-oracle after the free trial.

### Friction Points
- Cost of the tool.
- Concerns about the long-term effectiveness and scalability of the tool.

### User Emotions
- Relief about finding a solution to their pain point.
- Confidence in the tool's ability to improve their inventory management.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Competitive pricing compared to other inventory management solutions.
- Ongoing support and updates to ensure the tool remains effective and scalable.

### Metric
- Number of users who adopt stock-oracle.

## Expand

### Trigger Event
Adopted user expands their use of stock-oracle to additional fulfillment centers and vendors.

### Friction Points
- Complexity of integrating new fulfillment centers and vendors.
- Difficulty understanding how to optimize the tool for their specific needs.

### User Emotions
- Satisfaction with the tool's performance.
- Motivation to improve inventory management further.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Easy integration of new fulfillment centers and vendors.
- Customized support and guidance to optimize the tool for their specific needs.

### Metric
- Number of users who expand their use of stock-oracle.
